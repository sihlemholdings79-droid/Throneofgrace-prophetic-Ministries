Our Story

A Place of
Divine Encounters

Throne of Grace Prophetic Ministries is a sanctuary of divine encounters and spiritual growth, wholly dedicated to drawing people closer to God. We exist to ignite faith, nurture community, and release prophetic purpose in every life we touch.

Rooted in scripture and moved by the Spirit, we carry a heart for every soul — whether you are beginning your faith journey or deepening your walk with God. With three branches across Mpumalanga, we are committed to transforming lives through the power of His Word.

Our Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Prophet Micaiah Shepherd Madonsela Founder & Senior Prophet
✦

Prophet Micaiah
Shepherd Madonsela

Prophet Micaiah Shepherd Madonsela is the visionary founder of Throne of Grace Prophetic Ministries. Called by God with a burning mandate to awaken the prophetic voice across communities, he has built a ministry that stands as a beacon of hope, healing, and transformation.

Under his leadership, Throne of Grace has grown from a single gathering into a thriving ministry with three branches, impacting thousands of lives through the power of the Holy Spirit and the unwavering Word of God.

His ministry is marked by bold prophetic declarations, miraculous encounters, and an uncompromising devotion to God's Kingdom.
